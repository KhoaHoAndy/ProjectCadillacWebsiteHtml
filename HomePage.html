-Mission Statements and History

Established in 1987, Michael Boyer Chevrolet Cadillac Buick GMC Ltd has grown to become a premier Chevrolet, Cadillac, Buick, GMC auto dealer in the Durham region. Throughout our tenure we have strived to include the best possible customer service with top-notch repair/maintenance work and comprehensive new and used car, truck, minivan and SUV inventories.

Michael Boyer Chevrolet Cadillac Buick GMC Ltd was founded over 25 years ago by Michael E. Boyer. Based in PICKERING, Michael Boyer Chevrolet Cadillac Buick GMC Ltd has continued to expand as it now serves all of the greater Toronto area including Whitby, Ajax, Oshawa, Scarborough and all points in between with new and used Chevrolet, Cadillac, Buick GMC automobiles.

Over the years, Michael Boyer Chevrolet Cadillac Buick GMC Ltd has grown in its current location, as we now offer a state-of-the-art showroom, Parts & Service Centres and a Finance Department. Whether you are in need of a new or used Chevrolet, Cadillac, Buick GMC automobile or parts / service for your current vehicle, Michael Boyer Chevrolet Cadillac Buick GMC Ltd of PICKERING is here to assist you with any of your automotive needs!

At Michael Boyer Chevrolet Cadillac Buick GMC Ltd, we look forward to serving you, or valued customer, for years to come. Please stop by our dealership, located at 715 KINGSTON RD, PICKERING, ON and speak with one of our representatives today!
